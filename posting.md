#### Research topics typically include:
- The impact of U.S. interest rates or commodity prices on emerging economies and their currencies.
- The drivers behind Sub-Saharan Africa’s growth since 2000 — commodity cycles versus structural reforms.
- The implications of these changes for sovereign spreads and credit ratings.
